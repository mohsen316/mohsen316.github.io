What is Tragacanth Gum?

Tragacanth is a natural gum obtained from the root of the Astragalus plant, found in dry mountainous regions. It is hand-collected and sun-dried into unique ribbon or crystal shapes. Known for its purity, it is widely used in pharmaceuticals, foods, and cosmetics.

Product Form & Quality

We offer only the purest ribbon-grade tragacanth, carefully selected by hand and tested for quality. When hydrated, it becomes a soft, stable gel with unmatched natural thickening power.

Applications

Pharmaceutical

Used in syrups, herbal pastes, lozenges and tablet coatings as a safe natural stabilizer.

Food Industry

Acts as a clean-label thickener for sauces, ice cream, yogurt, and specialty drinks.

Cosmetics

Used in organic gels, hair care and facial creams for texture and stability.
