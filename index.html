About Sepidar

Sepidar Trade is dedicated to exporting top-quality tragacanth gum from Iran to global markets. We specialize in ribbon-grade tragacanth with full certifications and laboratory reports.

Iran supplies over 80% of the world’s tragacanth gum – and we are proud to bring this natural treasure to your hands.

Our gum is used in pharmaceutical syrups and tablets, natural foods, sauces, yogurt, and organic cosmetics. All products are carefully inspected, packed, and ready for export with full documentation.
